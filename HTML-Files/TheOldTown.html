The Old Town

The first thing you should visit when in Zurich

The Altstadt is the historical city of Zurich. This area belongs to the area of the city called Kreis 1 and it has an area of 1.8 km2. There are 12 districs in Zurich, Kreis 1 to Kreis 12. The Kreis 1 is divided in 4 parts: Rathaus, Hochschulen, Lindenhof and City. Only around 5500 people live in the Altstadt, that is about 1,5% of the total population of Zurich.
