Restaurant Italia

Bistro

This is a simple little bistro with a trendy and casual interior designg, proposing a very authentic, homemade and simple Italian cuisine. On the menu you can find a variaty of fresh pasta and meat but no Pizza is served here! They also offer an impressive wine list. The price per plate rounds about 25-30CHF.
