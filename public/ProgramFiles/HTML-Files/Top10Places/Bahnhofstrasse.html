Bahnhofstrasse

The first thing you should visit when in Zurich

Bahnhofstrasse is the main downtown street of Zurich. It is 1,4km and connects the main railway station and the zurich lake. This street is one of the most famous and expensive shoopping avenues in the world. You will find paradeplatz halfway between the station and the lake. this is the Swiss banking center, where the major Swiss banks set up their main offices.
