Uetliberg

The first thing you should visit when in Zurich

The Uetliberg is a mountain in Zurich situated between the city of Zurich and Stallikon and Uitikon. It has a heigh of 870m above sea level and offers an amazing view of the city. This is a great place for going on a hike, there are numerous hicking routes and mountain bke routes. By foot it will take about an hour to get to the top. Once you get there you will find an observation tower. It costs 2 Fr per person to get on this tower but it is definitely worth it.
